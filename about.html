About the Festival

R&J Dance Festival is an outdoor international show for a very passionate audience. Working nationally and internationally, this festival guarantees a good experience. This contemporary performance is about the real passion and true story of Romeo and Juliet, our best dancers will create an intense and fun atmosphere with their passionate moves. Come and discover the real story, and do not forget to dance with passion!
